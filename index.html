About-VIHUNG

VIHUNG-2K18 a Venue provided to Inspire and ignite the Hilarious minds of youth to Unveil their Neo technological innovations in the present Global scenario. The main objective of this techno-fest is to bring the hidden talents and skills in budding technocrats and emerging business administrative managers and to encourage creativity. The main objective of this techno-fest is to focus the skills of present day students suitable to the needs of tomorrow society.
